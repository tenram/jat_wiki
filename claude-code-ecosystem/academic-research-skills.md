## Overview

A Claude Code skill suite covering the full research pipeline (literature review through publication) via multi-agent teams, explicitly designed around human oversight rather than full automation.

## Components

Four skills: Deep Research (13-agent team, 8 modes including Socratic guided research and PRISMA systematic reviews), Academic Paper (12-agent writing pipeline, multiple structures and citation formats), Academic Paper Reviewer (7-agent peer review with 0-100 rubrics), and Academic Pipeline (10-stage orchestrator linking research to publication).

Notable safeguards: non-bypassable "stage integrity gates" at stages 2.5 and 4.5, a "concession threshold protocol" meant to make devil's-advocate debater agents resist sycophancy, and a "Material Passport" tracking experiment provenance and claim verification. Output formats include Markdown, DOCX, LaTeX (APA/IEEE/Chicago), and bilingual English/Traditional Chinese abstracts.

Stated philosophy directly counters fully-autonomous research framings elsewhere in this wiki (compare [Autoresearch](../research-and-training/autoresearch.md)): "AI augments researcher decision-making rather than acting autonomously."
