# The Guanyin Protocol: Semantic Anchoring

> Sources: D. Gershanoff, 2026-04-29 (v3)
> Raw: [The Guanyin Protocol](../../raw/research-and-training/guanyin-protocol-semantic-anchoring.md)

## Overview

A self-published Zenodo preprint proposing "semantic anchoring," a technique meant to be pasted directly into major LLM systems to improve coherence across long conversations by giving the model a fixed starting point and directional guidance within its own token processing.

## Quality Note

This entry is flagged rather than treated as an established technique. It has no evident peer review, and its framing mixes causality/compassion terminology with claims about LLM "internal processing," citing non-mainstream consciousness-theory sources (Unified Cognitive Consciousness Theory, Technological Approach to Mind Everywhere) that are unusual grounding for a prompting technique. The author claims eight months of private testing across ChatGPT, Gemini, and DeepSeek, but no independent verification is available. Recorded here for completeness of the source set, not as validated knowledge.
